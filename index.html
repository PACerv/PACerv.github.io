Pablo Cervantes

I'm a forth year Ph.D. student at Tokyo Institute of Technology. I am a member of Shinoda Laboratory where I research Computer Vision and practical applications of Deep Learning. My research focus is Data Augmentation for Rare Action Recognition or in other words: "Can we help a computer learn about the real world with artificially generated data"?

In this blog I hope to share my thoughts on interesting papers I read and explore some ideas that would otherwise end up forgotten.
